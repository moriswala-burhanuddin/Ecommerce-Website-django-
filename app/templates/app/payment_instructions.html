Payment Instructions

To complete your order with Burhani, please carefully follow these payment instructions to ensure a smooth transaction:

  1. Make your payment using the provided QR code. Simply scan the QR code with your mobile payment app to initiate the transaction.
  2. After completing the payment, please ensure to copy the transaction ID. This unique identifier is crucial for verifying your payment.
  3. Enter the transaction ID in the provided field on our website. This step is essential to link your payment to your order.
Please note: If you provide an incorrect transaction ID, your order will not be confirmed, and you may incur a fine. To avoid any issues, double-check the transaction ID before submitting it.
